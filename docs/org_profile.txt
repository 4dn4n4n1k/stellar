Who We Are 
 
Stellar Associates is a Bangladeshi development consulting firm established by five experienced 
development professionals with extensive backgrounds in democracy, governance, humanitarian 
and emergency assistance, labor rights, inclusive development, civic engagement, and private 
sector collaboration. With decades of combined experience working with USAID, international 
donors, government agencies, CSOs, and global private sector platforms, the Stellar team brings 
a unique blend of programmatic expertise, strategic advisory capacity, and operational 
excellence. 
A multidisciplinary team of five partners with higher degrees from Bangladesh and abroad and 
extensive experience working with USAID, UN, international donors, diplomatic missions, 
government ministries, industry associations (BGMEA, BKMEA, FBCCI, AmCham), and civil 
society organizations. 
Positioned at the intersection of policy, practice, and innovation, Stellar Associates aims to 
advance evidence-based, inclusive, and sustainable solutions to Bangladesh’s development 
challenges. The firm specializes in transforming insights into actionable strategies, delivering 
high-quality research, program design, capacity building, and implementation support tailored to 
the needs of diverse stakeholders. 
Stellar is committed to being a platform for technology-savvy, gender-responsive, rights-
sensitive, and environmentally conscious professionals dedicated to accelerating positive social 
change. 
Our Vision 
To become Bangladesh’s leading home-grown development consultancy firm that drives 
democratic governance, human rights, and inclusive socio-economic progress by connecting 
global standards with local expertise. 
Our Mission 
To support government, civil society, private sector, and development partners through high-
quality research, strategic advisory, program implementation support, and capacity-building 
services that promote accountability, inclusion, and sustainable development. 
 
Core Values 
● Integrity & Accountability: We commit to ethical, transparent, and responsible 
practices. 
● Inclusion: We advocate for the participation of women, youth, disadvantaged, and 
marginalized communities. 
● Innovation: We integrate digital tools, evidence, and modern methodologies into 
development solutions. 
● Collaboration: We build partnerships founded on trust and shared learning. 
● Quality & Professionalism: We deliver reliable, timely, and results-oriented services.
