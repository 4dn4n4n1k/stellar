Based on the organizational profile you shared, Stellar Associates should position itself online as 
a boutique international development consultancy and research firm, rather than a 
traditional consulting company. The website should communicate three messages immediately: 
1. Technical Excellence – research, assessments, MEL, and strategic advisory services.  
2. Credibility & Experience – senior experts with donor, UN, and international 
development backgrounds.  
3. Local Presence with International Standards – strong Bangladesh-based 
implementation capacity combined with global analytical expertise.  
For a newly established firm, I would recommend a clean, professional, donor-oriented 
website with 8–10 pages, rather than a large corporate site.

My recommendation 
For a new consultancy like Stellar Associates, the website should look closer to international 
firms such as: 
● Development Alternatives Inc. (DAI)  
● Tetra Tech International Development  
● Social Impact  
● Integrity Global  
● Ecorys  
● NIRAS  
The emphasis should be on expertise, evidence, and credibility, not on flashy graphics. A 
clean, text-rich website with strong expert profiles, service descriptions, and publications will be 
far more effective in attracting GIZ, EPD, UN agencies, USAID implementers, and international 
NGOs than a highly decorative corporate-style website.
